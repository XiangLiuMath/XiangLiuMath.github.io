01 / About

Mathematics for meaningful, real-world questions.

I work at the intersection of topological data analysis, topological deep learning, and mathematical AI. My goal is to develop principled learning methods that capture the structure hidden inside complex data.

My current work spans biomedical imaging, molecular science, drug design, and network systems.
